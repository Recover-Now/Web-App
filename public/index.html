Welcome to RecoverNow

Tinder for disaster relief! (now with NFC support!)

RecoverNow is a platform to connect victims of a local emergency to those wanting to provide support in the community. Here you can offer support, supplies and services; or find something you may need to help recover during a difficult time.

How to help:

Food and Water

Surplus food and water always comes in handy during emergencies.

Rides

Have a car? Offer rides to those displaced by disaster.

Are you a certified doctor?

You can offer basic medical support or find those in need of medical attention here

Housing

If you have extra space in your home, you may find those in need of a place to stay on RecoverNow
